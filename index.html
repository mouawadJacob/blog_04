The State of Virtual Reality in 2023

https://www.testdevlab.com/blog/the-state-of-virtual-reality-in-2023 January 5th, 2023

Virtual reality is the immersion of the brain in a virtual world. By using headsets, controllers, and a plethora of other more optional hardware pieces it allows the user to experience and usually interact with a completely different environment made in a virtual setting. Virtual reality or VR for short has been progressing at an astonishing rate in recent years. While it used to be primarily an outlet for entertainment through video games and videos; it has now branched out into fields like medicine and the automotive industry to help with the preparation of surgery and even the development of new cars and we can expect even more developments and new uses for VR in the different work fields. Now for some interesting statistics in relation to VR:
- “VR can reduce the risk of injury in the workplace by 43%” - “VR in e-commerce can boost online shopping conversion by 17%” - “1 in 3 users have experienced motion sickness of cybersickness during their VR experience”

Augmented reality or AR for short has been around along time. For example the Nintendo Dsi had games based around augmented reality, and that led to the birth of bigger more recent AR games like Pokemon go, or the new Monster Hunter now. But what is AR and what uses does it have outside of gaming, or entertainment. AR is a concept linked but almost opposite to VR. Instead of putting the mind into a virtual environment AR takes a virtual object and places it in the real world. Now with all the rapid advances in the tech field AR has become an important part of our world with uses outside of the entertainment world, and into the fields of education, retail, and even medicine, just to list a few. As for how it impacts them it can allow students to have manipulatable 3d models. Buyers can see what products look like on them or in their home before purchasing products. And Surgeons use AR to give themselves a headsup display of any vital information they may need throughout their procedure. The state of AI in 2023: Generative AI’s breakout year

https://www.mckinsey.com/capabilities/quantumblack/our-insights/the-state-of-ai-in-2023-generative-ais-breakout-year August 1st, 2023

Generative AI or gen AI for short is a form of AI that gives responses to inputs with text, images, or other outlets using the input training it has been put through to give those answers based on the patterns it can find between the input and its data base. In the last year the useage of gen AI has grown by an astronomical amount. being used in almost every industry by at least one of the business departments it is no longer just a program used in the tech industry. however, it is not yet right to fully trust the answers given out by these programs, and many of these companies need to make better efforts to prepare for the potential risks these programs can cause. for example, misinformation given from not proofing the answers given by the AI before moving ahead with using their inputs and outputs. as well as other risks like cybersecurity, or copyright infingments. for more info on this topic click on the link to the full article above.
